Our Customization Technologies

We use three main technologies to bring your branding vision to life on any surface

Laser Engraving

Best for premium, textured finishes
  • Bamboo Cutting Boards & Cups
  • Wooden Business Cards
  • Desk Name Plates
  • Coaster Sets (4 Pieces)
  • Leatherette Patch Hats
  • Kitchenware & Accessories

Pricing: Based on design size & material

DTF Printing

Best for vibrant, full-color apparel
  • Branded T-Shirts (Cotton/Poly)
  • Premium Hoodies & Sweatshirts
  • Canvas Tote Bags
  • Workwear Overalls
  • DTF Transfers (A4 Sheets)
  • All fabric-based items

Note: Bulk orders receive significant discounts

UV Light Printing

Best for durable hard surfaces
  • Executive Branded Pens
  • Custom Phone Cases
  • Branded Power Banks
  • Acrylic Awards & Trophies
  • Coffee & Beer Mugs
  • Office Signage & Plaques

Application: Virtually any hard surface

Why Prices Vary

Every project is unique. Here are the factors that influence your final quote:

Quantity

More items = Better price per unit. Bulk orders receive the best rates.

Design Complexity

Simple logo vs. full-color artwork. Complex designs require more setup.

Materials

Wood, fabric, metal, glass - each material has different costs and processing requirements.

Timeline

Standard turnaround vs. express service. Faster delivery may affect pricing.
